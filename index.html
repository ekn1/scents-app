Rescorla-Wagner RL — Attention Model
The engine learns which insight types you engage with and re-weights your intelligence feed accordingly. Each tap on an insight updates the associative strength V using the Rescorla-Wagner update rule.
ΔV = α·β·(λ − V)
α = salience · β = learn rate · λ = max value · V = current strength

Instagram-Style Conformal Ranking
Every insight is scored by relevance × recency × relationship strength, then ranked with a conformal coverage guarantee — higher-ranked insights are provably more actionable.
score(i) = w₁·P(relevant) + w₂·recency + w₃·V(category)
w₁=0.5 · w₂=0.2 · w₃=0.3 · V from Rescorla-Wagner
